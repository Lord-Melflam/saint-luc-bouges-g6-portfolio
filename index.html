Global problematic

Hospital discharge management is currently fragmented across units, creating bed unavailability peaks, delayed transfers, and high operational uncertainty. Our goal is to design an integrated solution that anticipates departures earlier, harmonizes planning, and organizes post-discharge transition without loss of care quality.
