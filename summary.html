Problem and approach

The project addresses discharge management inefficiencies at Saint-Luc Bouges. We progressively reframed the broad challenge into three focused streams: upstream (anticipation and communication), during (planning harmonization), and downstream (post-discharge transition). This decomposition improved clarity, ownership, and implementation feasibility.

Week-by-week progression

W1-W3 established design-thinking foundations and stakeholder mapping. W4-W7 produced core project deliverables, problem framing, and ideation outputs. W9-W10 consolidated financial/regulatory context and strategic split into three sub-problems. W11 focused on stream-level implementation and portfolio consolidation (including portfolio iteration 2).

Annex references are listed in the dedicated Annexes page for direct download and traceability.

Current result

Upstream and During streams are completed at concept/prototype integration level. Downstream (Maurine) and Lheo's integration stream are currently in progress. The portfolio now supports filtered weekly tracking and annex pointers for submission-ready traceability.
